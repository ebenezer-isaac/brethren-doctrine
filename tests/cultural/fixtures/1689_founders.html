The 1689 Baptist Confession of Faith in Modern English

This title can be purchased in print form in either a paperback edition or premium leather edition from Founders Press. For a discussion of how robust a confession of faith should be, see B.H. Carroll and Robust Confessionalism. For a suggestion about how a church might subscribe to the Second London Confession of Faith, see How to Subscribe to the Second London Baptist Confession of Faith. To understand the importance of creedalism, or confessionalism, see The Creedal Imperative by Carl Trueman.
